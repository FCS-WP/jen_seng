Our Heritage

A Family Tradition Since 1993

Our story began when two sisters and a spouse opened a small traditional medicine hall in Serangoon — full of heart, herbs, and the belief that good health starts with care.

"Every jar of herbs, every remedy, and every conversation carried the warmth of family and the belief that good health starts with care."

Through three decades and two relocations, the family's promise has never changed: honest remedies, friendly service, and care that comes from the heart.
